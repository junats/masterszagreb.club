# TrueTrack: Application Feature Overview

## 🚀 Mission
**"Financial Neutrality for Modern Families"**
TrueTrack is built to empower single parents and co-parents with unbiased, verifiable financial record-keeping. It reduces conflict by replacing "he-said-she-said" arguments with data.

---

## 💰 Core Budgeting & Expense Tracking
*   **Smart Dashboard:** Instantly visible "Left to Spend" amount with a visual progress ring.
*   **Monthly Budget Control:** Simple slider to set monthly limits (e.g., €500).
*   **Category Breakdowns:** Tracks spending across 10+ categories (Food, Dining, Necessity, Transport, Education, etc.).
*   **Visual Insights:** 
    *   **Spending Distribution:** Donut charts showing exactly where money goes.
    *   **Weekly Trends:** Bar charts comparing spending habits over time.
    *   **"Stop Light" Indication:** Visual cues (Green/Red) indicating if you are on track or over budget.

## 🧾 AI Receipt Scanning (The "Receipt Radar")
*   **Instant Scan:** Snap a photo of any receipt.
*   **Gemini AI Intelligence:** 
    *   **Auto-Categorization:** Automatically sorts items into "Child Related", "Alcohol", "Luxury", "Necessity", etc.
    *   **Line-Item Extraction:** Doesn't just read the total; extracts every single item (milk, bread, diapers) individually.
    *   **Restricted Item Detection:** Flags "Red Flag" items like Alcohol or Gambling for awareness.
    *   **Child Expense Detection:** Automatically tags items like "Diapers", "Tuition", "Toys" for co-parenting reports.
*   **Duplicate Protection:** Prevents scanning the same receipt twice to ensure accurate records.

## 🤝 Co-Parenting & Single Parent Support
*   **Custody Calendar:** 
    *   Track "Mom Days" vs "Dad Days" visually.
    *   Log drop-offs, pickups, and special events.
*   **Direct Sync:** Securely syncs data between parents (if connected) to share custody schedules.
*   **Provision Analysis (Who Pays What):**
    *   Calculates the "True Cost of the Child".
    *    compares % of Custody Time vs % of Expenses Paid.
    *   **Fairness Gauge:** Visually displays if the financial burden is shared fairly based on custody time.
*   **Legal-Ready Exports:** 
    *   Generate PDF reports of all child-related expenses.
    *   Perfect for mediation, court hearings, or child support assessments.

## 🧘 Wellness & Lifestyle
*   **"Ambient Mode":** A calming, dynamic background that changes color based on your spending health (Green = Good, Red = Caution).
*   **Habit Tracking:** Set goals to reduce "Junk Food", "Smoking", or "Impulse Buying".
*   **Streak Counter:** Gamifies good financial habits with "Days Clean" streaks.

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
*   **Apple ID Login:** Secure, private authentication (supports "Hide My Email").
*   **Supabase Backup:** Data is securely encrypted and backed up to the cloud.
*   **Local-First Design:** Works offline (mostly) and syncs when online.

---

## 🛠 Target Audience: Single & Co-Parents
*   **The Problem:** Arguments over money ("I paid for school shoes!", "No you didn't!").
*   **The Solution:** TrueTrack provides an immutable record. "Here is the receipt, date, and item. It is verified."
*   **The Vibe:** Calm, Neutral, Empowering. Not a strict "accounting" app, but a "peace of mind" app.
