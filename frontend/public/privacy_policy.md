# Privacy Policy for TrueTrack

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

## 1. Introduction
TrueTrack ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application and services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and profile picture (optional) provided during registration.
- **Financial Data:** We store data you input about expenses, receipts, and budgets.
- **Receipt Images:** Photos of receipts you upload or scan.

### B. Usage Data
- We gather non-identifiable usage data (e.g., features used, crash logs) to improve app performance.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
- **Service Provision:** To provide expense tracking, budget management, and custody schedule features.
- **AI Analysis:** We use Google Gemini AI to analyze receipt images and extract data (date, merchant, total). **Receipt images are processed securely and are not used to train public AI models.**
- **Communication:** To send important account notifications (e.g., password resets).

## 4. Data Storage and Security
- **Storage:** Data is stored securely on Supabase servers (hosted on AWS).
- **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and at rest.
- **Biometrics:** If you enable FaceID/TouchID, biometric data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

## 5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Service Providers:** Supabase (database), Google Cloud (AI processing).
- **Legal Compliance:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Your Rights
- **Access & Correction:** You can view and edit your data within the app settings.
- **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data directly within the app (Settings > Account > Delete Account). This action is permanent.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
