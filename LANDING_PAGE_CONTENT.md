## 2. The Core Problem & Our Solution

**Problem:**
Traditional budgeting apps are too generic. They don't account for the complexity of co-parenting, they don't help you build discipline habits, and they certainly don't provide "legal-ready" evidence of your financial support.

**Solution:**
TrueTrack bridges the gap between financial tracking and life discipline. It combines AI-powered scanning with specialized co-parenting widgets to prove your provisions and master your budget.

## 3. High-Impact Feature Cards

### 📱 Smart AI Scanning
*   **Gemini-Powered OCR:** Effortlessly scan receipts and bills. Automatically detects store names, dates, items, and tax.
*   **Duplicate Protection:** Never double-count. Our signature engine detects and flags duplicate uploads instantly.
*   **HEIC Native:** optimized for iPhone camera uploads.

### 📊 The Bento Dashboard
*   **Spending Distribution:** Beautiful, interactive pie charts showing where every cent goes.
*   **Total Visibility:** Side-by-side comparison of overall expenses vs. child-related spending.
*   **Tap for Detail:** Tappable charts open glassmorphism detail popups with full legends and category bars.

### ⚖️ Legal-Ready Evidence
*   **Evidence Score:** A gamified 0-100 metric tracking your log consistency and provision ratio.
*   **One-Click Export:** Generate structured CSV/PDF reports containing itemized lists, dates, and prices—ready for counsel or mediation.
*   **Verified Provision:** Tag specific items as "Child/Home Needs" to prove your financial contribution levels.

## 4. Specialized Co-Parenting Suite

### **Co-Parenting Widget Rework**
*   **GitHub-Style Grid:** A heat map of your custody days across the month.
*   **Orbital Ring Chart:** A circular donut visualization of your custody distribution.
*   **DNA Helix Pattern:** An animated visualization of your custody rhythm.
*   **AI Smart Insights:** 10+ contextual insights flagging spending correlations, transition reminders, and balance alerts.

### **Parental Protection Mode**
*   **18+ Item Filtering:** Bought a beer with the groceries? Parental Mode allows you to omit restricted items from shared reports to prevent friction, while keeping your personal budget accurate.
